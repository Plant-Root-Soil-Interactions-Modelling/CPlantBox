
### CPlantBox WebApp

Showcases the capabilities of [CPlantBox](https://github.com/Plant-Root-Soil-Interactions-Modelling/CPlantBox), and provides an interactive interface to visualize and experiment with plant structures directly in your browser.

**Citation:** If you use CPlantBox in your research, please cite:

M Giraud, SL Gall, M Harings, M Javaux, D Leitner, F Meunier, Y Rothfuss, D van Dusschoten, J Vanderborght, H Vereecken, et al. CPlantBox: a fully coupled modelling platform for the water and carbon fluxes in the soil–plant–atmosphere continuum. *in silico Plants*, 5(2):diad009, 2023.
